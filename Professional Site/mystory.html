My Journey from Germany to Cape Town to BYU

A Story of Family, Passion, and Swimming

Peter Skye Connor Etzold, Born on June 14, 2005

I was born in Germany but spent most of my childhood in Cape Town, South Africa. Moving to Cape Town as a child was a significant change, but it shaped who I am today. The beauty of the city, the diverse culture, and the opportunities to excel in sports became an integral part of my life.

Discovering My Passion for Swimming

Growing up near the ocean and having access to incredible swimming facilities allowed me to develop a deep love for swimming. From a young age, I competed in local and national competitions, striving to improve with each race. Swimming taught me discipline, resilience, and the importance of teamwork.

Chasing New Horizons

My dedication to swimming and academics led me to incredible opportunities. I completed my education at the German International School in Cape Town, earning a scholarship for both athletics and academics. During this time, I dreamed of combining my passion for swimming with higher education.

That dream became a reality when I was offered a scholarship to join the NCAA Division I Swim Team at Brigham Young University. BYU not only gave me the chance to continue my swimming career but also pursue my academic goals in business.

Why BYU?

Choosing BYU was an easy decision. The university's focus on excellence, both in athletics and academics, aligns perfectly with my values. Being part of the swim team here has allowed me to grow as an athlete and as a person. The challenges I face in the pool and the classroom continue to push me toward becoming the best version of myself.

"Success is not the destination but the journey."

Looking Ahead

My story is far from over. Every day, I am motivated by the lessons I learned growing up in Cape Town and the opportunities I now have at BYU. I look forward to continuing to improve, both in the pool and in my studies, as I work toward a successful future.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about my journey!
